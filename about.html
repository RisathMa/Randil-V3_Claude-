Our Story

A Decade of
Loving Care

Indusarani Montessori House & Daycare Center was founded with one simple belief: every child deserves to be cared for with as much love as their own family would give. With over 10 years of experience in Gampaha, we have grown into a trusted cornerstone of early childhood education in the Western Province.

Our Montessori-inspired approach places the child at the center of their own learning journey. We believe children are naturally curious and capable — our role is to create the right environment for that curiosity to flourish.

Mission, Vision & Philosophy

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a safe, loving, and stimulating environment for early childhood growth — where every child is treated as our own and encouraged to reach their full potential.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the most trusted childcare and preschool center in Gampaha — recognized for excellence in early childhood education, safety, and parent partnership.

🌿

Our Philosophy

We follow the Montessori philosophy — child-centered, play-based learning that respects each child's individual pace and nurtures independence, creativity, and joy.
