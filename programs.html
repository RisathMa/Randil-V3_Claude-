Montessori Preschool

Ages 2–6 years | Monday–Saturday | 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Our Montessori preschool program is the heart of Indusarani. Based on Dr. Maria Montessori's philosophy, we create a prepared, child-centered environment where children learn through exploration, discovery, and hands-on activities. Each child moves at their own natural pace with personalized guidance from our experienced educators.

Children develop independence, concentration, coordination, and order — skills that form the foundation of all future learning. Our rich classroom materials span language, mathematics, sensorial, practical life, and cultural studies.

✓ Montessori-certified learning materials
✓ Language & literacy development
✓ Mathematics & numeracy foundations
✓ Sensorial exploration activities
✓ Practical life skills
✓ Motor skills (fine & gross)
✓ Cognitive development
✓ School readiness preparation
✓ Social & emotional learning
✓ Personalized learning pace
